Four integrated modules

One system. Four intelligences.

01 / DISEASE PREDICTION

Constitutional-Aware Prediction

Predicts diseases using ML classifiers that integrate symptoms with Prakriti and tridosha imbalance — same body, smarter context.

02 / MEDICINE

Herbal Recommendation

NER-driven herb suggestions, classified by Random Forest and SVM models considering dosha, age, severity, and contraindications.

03 / DIET

Dietary Planning

Personalized meal plans by combining Ayurvedic principles with modern nutrition considering dosha, disease, BMI and dietary preference to recommend suitable foods with nutritional values and Ayurvedic classifications.

04 / Q&A

Source-Grounded Q&A

Multi-script OCR + hybrid RAG over Charaka Samhita and Madhava Nidana — answers in Sinhala, English, or Singlish, with verse citations.

Why this research

The gap between
tradition and access.

Sri Lankans deeply value Ayurveda, yet qualified practitioners are scarce in many regions — pushing people toward unverified online advice and self-medication. Modern healthcare has embraced AI; traditional medicine has not.

This project closes that gap by building a reliable, educational digital companion that respects the depth of Ayurvedic principles while making them accessible.

  • Limited practitioner access

    Especially in rural areas, in-person consultations remain difficult to obtain.

  • Constitution is overlooked

    Existing systems map symptoms to diseases without considering individual Prakriti.

  • Unstructured classical knowledge

    Charaka Samhita and Madhava Nidana hold rich symptom–disease relationships in unstructured Sanskrit text.

  • Trust and transparency

    Users deserve to understand why a recommendation was made — not just receive it.
